Demonstration of viewBox, preserveAspectRatio, and transform attributes on the scaling of SVG elements.

Each SVG has the same graphics content:

  • a 5px(user coordinates) thick red rectangle drawn from (0,0) with 100% width and 100% height;
  • points drawn at the listed locations in user coordinates.

Each SVG element has a 2px blue border and is 150px tall.
